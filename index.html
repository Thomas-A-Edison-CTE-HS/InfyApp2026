About the Challenge

Build. Test. Pitch. Reflect.

Students research a social issue, design a useful app, meet weekly milestones, gather feedback, and present a working final product.

🎯 Goals

Each team sets technical and personal learning goals connected to their product idea.

🤝 Mentorship

Teams prepare agendas, ask better questions, and use mentor feedback to improve their app.

🚀 Final Deliverable

Every team presents a working app, prototype, or demo that addresses a real-world issue.
