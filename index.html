About the Challenge

Empathize. Define. Ideate. Build. Test. Pitch. Reflect.

Students investigate a real-world social issue, apply Human-Centered Design principles to develop a digital solution (app or game), complete weekly project milestones, incorporate peer and mentor feedback, and deliver a functional final product through a professional presentation and prototype showcase.

🎯 Innovation Goals

Each team develops technical, creative, and problem-solving goals aligned with their social impact app or game concept.

🤝 Industry Mentorship

Teams collaborate with mentors, prepare professional updates, ask meaningful questions, and apply feedback to strengthen their product design and development process.

🚀 Showcase Presentation

Every team delivers a polished prototype, professional pitch, and interactive demo that presents a technology-driven solution to a real-world challenge.
